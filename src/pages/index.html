Hello! I am a user experience designer specialising in the public sector. I currently work in the government, supporting a range of public agencies to design & deliver better digital products.

I hold a Bachelors in Social Psychology and Urban Studies, and a Masters in Political Science. Designing for the public good is the perfect intersection of these passions.

I am always seeking to apply my knowledge of behavioural sciences & experimental methods to the design process. I occasionally write about these explorations.
