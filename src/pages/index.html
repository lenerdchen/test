Hello there! I am a user experience designer specialising in the public sector. I currently work in the government, helping a range of public agencies to design & deliver better public digital services.

I hold a Bachelors in Social Psychology and Urban Politics, and a Masters in Political Science. Designing for the public good is the perfect intersection of these passions.

I am always looking to integrate my knowledge & method of behavioural sciences to the art of human-centred design. I occasionally write about these explorations.
